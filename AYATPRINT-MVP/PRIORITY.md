# AYATPRINT VISION 2.0 — TECHNICAL PRIORITIZATION MATRIX
**Prepared by**: Chief Creative Director & AI Lead  
**Date**: March 2026  
**Status**: Completed  

---

This priority matrix defines the critical path for the engineering team. Every feature must trace back to the user's **Vision 2.0 Manifesto**.

---

## 🟥 PRIORITY 0: CRITICAL LAUNCH BLOCKERS (IMMEDIATE IMPLEMENTATION)

1.  **Luxury Editorial Gallery Shift**: Establish collections-first navigation, spacious negative-space layouts, and large room mockups. Remove generic, crowded grids.
2.  **AI Product Score Card Overlay**: Hovering analytics dashboard overlay on gallery product cards displaying Demand, Conversion, Sales velocity, Favorites, and Regional appeal.
3.  **AI Trend Intelligence Hub**: Connect `/api/trends` and `/api/creative-director` to a beautiful client dashboard. Show global Pinterest, Etsy, TikTok growth statistics, daily designer reports, and implement the **Dynamic AI Product Synthesis Engine** to instantly create and publish trend-modeled items.
4.  **Client Personal Account Vault**: Introduce custom drawers or views containing favorited wishlists, saved master designs, shipping tracking timelines, invoice generators, and high-res print-ready PDF download capabilities.
5.  **Figma-Style Canvas Workspace**: Restructure the Design Editor layout (70% Stage, 30% Control sidebar). Implement a zoom/pan toolbar and grid toggles. Add layer workspace locking/hiding controls.
6.  **Real-Time AI Design Advisor**: Smart floating assistant inside the editor suggesting font sizing, typography pairings, frame options, and matching palettes.
7.  **Luxury Mockup Environmental Suite**: Expand to 24+ custom mockup venues. Implement refinement filters (warmth, brightness, contrast, hanger offsets) and a safety pre-flight scanner.

---

## 🟨 PRIORITY 1: HIGH VALUE (IMMEDIATE IMPLEMENTATION)

8.  **Connected Channels Publisher**: Etsy SEO listing writer, Printify margin/profit cost calculators, and full synchronization checklists in `IntegrationsDrawer`.
9.  **Executive Store Performance Analytics**: Embed administrative dashboards featuring simulated total revenue, orders count, print fulfillment expenditures, top countries list, and scheduled automation background logs.
10. **Multilingual Smart Search**: Search bars with smart auto-suggest in English, Arabic, and French, with high-contrast UI and voice search simulations.
