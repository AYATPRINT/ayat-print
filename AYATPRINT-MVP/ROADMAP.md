# AYATPRINT VISION 2.0 — IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP
**Prepared by**: Chief Creative Director & AI Lead  
**Date**: March 2026  
**Status**: Completed  

---

This roadmap outlines the three strategic phases to migrate the AyatPrint platform into an elite AI-Powered Islamic Design House.

---

## 🗺️ PHASE 1: BRAND INTEGRITY & AI CREATIVE CORE (CURRENT COHORT)
*Target: Immediate Completion (P0-P1)*

*   **1.1. Luxury Editorial Experience**: Redesign `/src/components/Marketplace.tsx` from standard card lists into a breathtaking, spacious editorial showcase featuring collections-first navigation and large room mockup backdrops.
*   **1.2. Executive AI Trend Dashboard**: Establish the **Trend Intelligence Hub** to display real-time global demand signals (Pinterest, Google, TikTok), view the Daily AI Brief, and allow "one-click Synthesis" to instantly publish AI-modeled products.
*   **1.3. AI Product Analytics Overlay**: Implement a detailed hovering breakdown overlay on all gallery products displaying demand indexes, sales velocity, and regional fit.
*   **1.4. Canva-Figma Canvas Workspace**: Restructure the Design Editor to prioritize a 70% canvas focus area. Create a custom toolbar for zoom, pan, and gridlines. Establish a layer controller (hide/lock layout components).
*   **1.5. Mockup Environmental Suite**: Support 24+ custom environments with adjustments for warmth, brightness, contrast, and safe-margin pre-flight auditing.
*   **1.6. Real-time AI Design Advisor**: Integrate a smart sidebar assistant inside the editor suggesting typography pairings, color overlays, framing rules, and placement guides.
*   **1.7. Customer Personal Account Vault**: Introduce a client account drawer containing favorited wishlists, saved master templates, shipping tracking timelines, invoice generators, and high-res print-ready PDF download capabilities.

---

## 🗺️ PHASE 2: CHANNEL HARMONIZATION & EXECUTIVE CONTROL (NEXT RELEASE)
*Target: Post-Launch Expansion (P1)*

*   **2.1. Connected Store Publisher**: Implement full Etsy listing SEO publishers, Printify production/profit margin sliders, and shipping status checkers in `IntegrationsDrawer`.
*   **2.2. Executive Store Performance Analytics**: Embed high-fidelity revenue charts, fulfillment expenditure breakdowns, top customer regions lists, and background AI maintenance logs.
*   **2.3. Multilingual Search Optimization**: Expand the catalog search bar to support Arabic, English, and French auto-suggestions, autocomplete, and mock Voice Recognition searches.

---

## 🗺️ PHASE 3: METALLIC REALISM & DEEP WORKSPACE PLUGINS (FUTURE MILESTONES)
*Target: Long-Term Horizon (P2-P3)*

*   **3.1. Shimmering Gold Foil Rendering**: Integrate WebGL or advanced SVG shaders in `CanvasStage` to render dynamic shimmering metallic gold reflections on scroll/hover.
*   **3.2. Google Workspace Sheets & Drive Automation**: Enable automated, scheduled exports of trending product configurations, metadata, and 300 DPI high-res PDFs straight into Google Drive folders for contract print shops.
*   **3.3. Multi-user Live Collaboration**: Enable real-time shared canvases allowing family members in different countries to collaboratively adjust font arrangements, translations, and frame shapes.
